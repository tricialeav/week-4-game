Star Wars

A Role Playing Game

You will be able to choose one of four characters as your avatar. Once chosen, you will need to defeat the three remaining characters to win.

Player damage has two components: base roll and attack power. Base roll is calculated each time the player attacks, and attack power is a multiplier that increases base roll on each hit.

Your opponent's damage also has two components: base power and counter attack. Counter attack, though initially greater than attack power, does not increase during play.
